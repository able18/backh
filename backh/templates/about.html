Backhome organization

The organization deals with education, health and environmental conservation. The organization supports children from poor families to give them exercise books, pencil, pens, rubber and d-light solar. These all is through donation. Also the organization will find sponsors to support them in their academic carrier.

The organization provide free menstrual health education to girls especially in rural areas. Girls in rural areas sometimes stay a week without attending classes due to menstrual period and they don't know what to do. The organization provides free sanitary pads and instruct them on how to use and make them comfortable when they are in menstrual period.

The organization also supports poor families by provide medical insurance. Most rural families are not able to get medical services due to poverty. This is through finding sponsors who will support poor families.
